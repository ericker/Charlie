Spatial memory test, part 1 (practice)

On the next screen you will see some squares. A
circle will appear in some of the squares. Your task
is to click on the squares that contained the
circles. Start by clicking on the square that first
contained the circle, then the square that next
contained the circle, etc.

Press any key to begin a practice trial.
---
Spatial memory test, part 1 (main test)

Now you will perform the real test. It is the same
as the practice. Try to complete the task as quickly
as you can without making mistakes.

Press any key to begin.
---
Spatial memory test, part 2 (practice)

This part of the test is the same as before, except
that now you must respond in reverse order. Start by
clicking on the square that last contained the
circle, then the square that contained the circle
second-to-last, etc.

Press any key to begin a practice trial.
---
Spatial memory test, part 2 (main test)

Now you will perform the real test. It is the same
as the practice. Try to complete the task as quickly
as you can without making mistakes.

Press any key to begin.
---
Spatial memory test, part 3 (practice)

Now, a circle or an X will appear in the squares.
Your task is to click on the squares that contained
the circle, in order, and then click on the squares
that contained the X, in order.

Press any key to begin a practice trial.
---
Spatial memory test, part 3 (main test)

Now you will perform the real test. It is the same
as the practice. Try to complete the task as quickly
as you can without making mistakes.

Press any key to begin.
