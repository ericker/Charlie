Emotion Recognition Test.

On each trial you will hear a person's voice. Your
task is to judge how the person is feeling by
clicking on the correct word. They will be either
happy, sad, angry, afraid, or neutral (no
emotion). If you are not sure, guess.

Press any key to begin.
