Test of facial memory, part 1 (learning phase)

There are two parts to this test. In the first part,
you will see images of people's faces, one at a time.
Try to remember each one. Later you will be asked to
recognize the faces you have seen.

Press any key to begin.
---
Test of facial memory, part 1 (test phase)

In this second part, you will see some more images of
people's faces. Some of these faces you have already
seen and others are new. Respond 'Yes' if you have
seen the face before, or 'No' if the face is new. If
you are not sure, guess. Try to complete the task as
quickly as you can without making mistakes.

Press any key to begin.
